Our Story

Born from a Love of Tea

SiphoraHq started with a simple belief — that the vessel you drink from matters as much as what's inside it. We set out to bring museum-quality porcelain into everyday Indian homes, without the museum price tag.

Based in Faridabad, Haryana, we work closely with ceramic artisans to create pieces that last a lifetime and look beautiful doing it.
